> [!IMPORTANT]  
> Function apps running the [end-of-life v3 runtime](../articles/azure-functions/functions-versions.md#retired-versions) on Linux in a Consumption plan stop running after September 30, 2026. To avoid service disruption, [migrate your app to the v4 runtime](../articles/azure-functions/migrate-version-3-version-4.md) and then to the [Flex Consumption plan](../articles/azure-functions/flex-consumption-plan.md).
